## 💬 Sample Customer Support Responses

### 1. Handling a Complaint

**Customer:** “I received a damaged product.”
**Response:**
Hi, I’m really sorry to hear that your product arrived damaged. I completely understand how frustrating this must be. Please share a picture of the item, and I will arrange a replacement or refund for you right away.

---

### 2. Late Delivery Issue

**Customer:** “My order hasn’t arrived yet.”
**Response:**
Hi, thank you for reaching out. I apologize for the delay in your order. Let me check the status for you right now. I’ll make sure this is resolved as quickly as possible and keep you updated.

---

### 3. General Inquiry

**Customer:** “Can you tell me more about your service?”
**Response:**
Hi! Thank you for your interest. I’d be happy to help. Our service offers [brief explanation]. If you have any specific questions, feel free to ask—I’m here to assist you.
